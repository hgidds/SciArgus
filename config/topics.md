#CCA-DRR integration
Either conceptual, or a case study approach, that looks at the interconnection between climate change adaptation and disaster risk. I want to know about benefits, challenges, trade-offs, barriers, approaches, methods, processes, frameworks. Actor network analysis of CCA-DRR integration governments. Critical policy reviews based on CCA-DRR integration. 

#Disaster risk reduction (DRR)
The historical development of the disaster risk reduction paradigm, its conceptual frameworks and theories, current and future DRR policies, processes and case studies focused within a governmental area. Criticisms and new theories, how DRR can be more systemic and address the root causes of risk rather than hazards alone. I am less interested in participatory methods and local level risk assessment, and more interested in the political governance / political ecology side of things where policy is created. I am interested in network analysis of institutions and process tracing of specific hazards or policies. I am interested in the interlinkages of DRR and climate change adaptation. This also includes references to Disaster Risk Management (DRM), and Emergency Management or IEM in some places. 

#Disaster risk creation (DRC)
New theories, ideas and concepts relating to disaster risk creation as opposed to disaster risk reduction. What constitutes DRC? Why is it better or worse than disaster risk reduction or disaster risk management? What are its main componenets? How does DRC fit into risk governance at the national or international levels? 

#Climate change adaptation (CCA)
The historical development of climate change adaptation, its conceptual frameworks and theories, current and future adaptation strategies including more adaptive governance methods such as dynamic adaptive policy pathways. Case studies of climate change adaptation governance strategies at national or regional levels. Criticisms and new theories / approaches, how CCA can be more systemic and address vulnerability. I am less interested in community based adaptation or ecosystem based adaptation approaches and more interested in political governance / political ecology side of things where policy is created. I am interested in network analysis of institutions and process tracing of specific hazards or policies. I am interested in the interlinkages of DRR and climate change adaptation. 

#Resilience
Research that considers the theory and concepts of resilience in relation to environmental hazards and risk. What critiques of resilience are given? What conditions are needed for its success? How do people feel about the label 'resilient' and are there better ways of vocalising what the academic community mean by resilience? What factors increase resilience? What trade offs are needed to be resilient? What barriers are there to making a community or area resilient? How is resilience used in national or international policy documents and frameworks and what are the critiques of these uses? I am not looking for resilience in relation to mental health, wellbeing, health, biology, ecology.

#Scottish environmental policy
Case studies of how Scottish environmental policy has been created, network analysis of actors involved in the policy creation. Critical analysis of existing policies. Environmental policy relates to hazards such as flooding, wildfires, earthquakes, landslides and landslips, subsurface risks, heavy rainfall, heatwaves, cold temperatures, storms, sea level rise, coastal erosion, high winds etc. Barriers, trade offs and challenges to policy creation. I am less interested in climate mitigation policy.

#Risk governance
Research that explores how risk is governance at national, regional and international levels. How are hazards, exposure, vulnerability, sensitivity and capacity considered? Who makes decisions? Who has power? How do decisions get made? Who is communicating and sharing information, finances and resource capacity? I am particularly interested in Global North governance but keen to learn from examples and case studies globally.

#Foresight analysis
The method of using foresight analysis or future scenario analysis to create possible futures and evaluate actions and outcomes, in relation to climate change adaptation or disaster risk management. Methodology and methods used, data collected, insights into the possible scenarios created, and in particular if any mixed methods approaches were used.
